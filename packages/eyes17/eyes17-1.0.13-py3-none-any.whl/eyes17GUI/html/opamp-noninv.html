Instructions

  • Use the Oscilloscope to test the Non-Inverting Amplifier.
  • Make the connections on a breadboard.
  • Enable A1, A2 and their analysis option
  • Set WG amplitude to 80 mV.
  • Set the range of A1 to 0.5 V and range of A2 to 4V.
  • Monitor the traces.
  • Change the gain by changing one of the resistors. Gain = 1 + Rf/Ri
