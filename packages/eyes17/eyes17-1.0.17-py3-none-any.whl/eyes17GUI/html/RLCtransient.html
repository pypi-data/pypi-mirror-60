Schematic

  • To study the transient response on a series LCR circuit, we connect them as shown in the figure and apply a zero to 5 volts step, and capture the resulting change in voltage across the capacitor.
  • There could be three types of responses; over damped, under damped or criticaly damped depending on the C/L ratio and value of R.

Instructions

  • Use a 0.1 uF capacitor and the coil provided with ExpEYES.
  • The result is under damped, it can be compared with the equation V(t) = V0 * sin(2pit) * exp(-dt), to obtain the resonant frequency and damping factor.
  • Add series resistor to increase damping.
  • Change C to 1uf and repeat.
