Schematic

  • Voltaic cells are based on electrochemical reaction. The reaction typically occurs between two pieces of metal, called electrodes, and a liquid or paste, called an electrolyte.
  • Many fruits and liquids can be used for the acidic electrolyte.
  • Copper and Zinc plates are used as electrodes.
